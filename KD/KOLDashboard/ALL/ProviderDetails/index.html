Provider Details

Displays Physician Details

Cohort Details

Shows selected Cohort and Cohort Name and its Description.

Provider Summary

Shows Number of Clinical Trials Done by the Provider, Number of sites the Provider worked, Count of Facility that the Provider working as Primary Provider, Number of Facility that the Provider is associated with and Number of Patients getting treated by the Provider for this Cohort, Patients Gender(split up), Provider Referral for Patients and Journal count for this Provider.

Top Diagnosis (MDCR Part B)

Shows Top 10 Medicare Part B Visits by Diagnosis.

Top Procedures (MDCR Part B)

Shows Top 10 Medicare Part B Visits by Procedure.

Top Diagnosis

Shows Top 10 Visits by Diagnosis.

Top Drug

Shows Top 10 Visits by Drug.

Top Procedure

Shows Top 10 Visits by Procedure.

Payer Channel

Shows Payer Channel along with their Visit Counts.
Note: Payer Channel information is not applicable for the Closed Claims.

Top Payer Rollup

Shows Number of Visits for each Payer that is rolled up to show the highest Visit counts(Top 10).Reactive to Payer Channel.
Note: Payer Rollup information is not applicable for the Closed Claims.

Top Payer Plans

Shows Top 10 Payers which holds highest Visit Counts. Reactive to Payer Channel and Top Payer Rollup.
Note: Payer Plan information is not applicable for the Closed Claims.

Patients by Age

Shows Patients treated by this Provider based on their Age Group.

Patients by Race

Shows Patients treated by this Provider based on their Race.
